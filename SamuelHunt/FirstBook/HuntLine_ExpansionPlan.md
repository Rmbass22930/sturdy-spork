# Hunt Line – Expansion Plan toward 400 Pages
Samuel Hunt Thriller Project

## Goal
Grow the initial ~9k-word manuscript into ~100k words (~400 printed pages) while preserving the hybrid Lee Child/Tom Clancy voice.

## Strategy
1. **Chapter Pairing:** Each current chapter expands into 2–3 scenes (approx. 4,500–5,000 words).  
2. **Interludes:** Add brief POV interludes (Maya Trask, Maverick, Leyla) between major chapters to deliver intel dumps and emotional beats. Limit to 300–600 words each.  
3. **Technical Inserts:** For Clancy flavor, include realistic descriptions (satellite tasking, reactor specs, weapon schematics) attached to key moments.  
4. **Character Arcs:** Deepen internal conflicts (Hunt’s burnout, Leyla’s secret network, Noura’s transformation) with flashbacks and moral dilemmas.  
5. **Subplots:** Introduce side missions (e.g., Barakat extraction prep, refugee convoy, Mossad double-cross) to pad length without filler.  
6. **Act Break Hooks:** End each act with a cliffhanger requiring 1–2 extra chapters before resolution.

## Scene Expansion Checklist
For each chapter:
1. **Cold Open (250–500 words):** Immediate tension hook (stakeout, memory, surveillance log).  
2. **Primary Action (2k words):** Core scene (fight, infiltration, intel chase).  
3. **Reflection/Intel (750 words):** Hunt processes fallout, interacts with Leyla/Trask.  
4. **Secondary Beat (1k words):** Subplot advancement (e.g., Noura’s perspective, Barakat prepping QRF).  
5. **Cliffhanger (250 words):** Tease next chapter.
