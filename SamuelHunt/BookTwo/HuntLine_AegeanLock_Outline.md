# Hunt Line Book Two – "Aegean Lock" Outline

## High-Level Premise
- Maverick resurfaces in Greece, partnering with rogue Hellenic Navy officer Cmdr. Nikos Vartholomeos and Russian oligarch Lev Orlov.
- Operation **Aegean Lock** hides micro-reactors inside decommissioned NATO facilities on Greek islands to blackmail EU energy markets.
- Samuel Hunt, Leyla Farouq, Noura al-Hadid, and new allies race across Athens, Cyclades, and Crete to dismantle the plot.

## Key Players
- **Samuel Hunt:** ex-MP, analyst-warrior hybrid.
- **Leyla Farouq:** art smuggler/fixer.
- **Noura al-Hadid:** nuclear engineer launching Cedar Coalition.
- **Daphne Markou:** NIS cyber analyst, conflicted patriot.
- **Dr. Aris Papadopoulos:** Greek nuclear physicist.
- **Jenny Stavros:** ex-Olympic sailor turned smuggler.
- **Cmdr. Nikos Vartholomeos:** rogue naval officer running Aegean Lock.
- **Lev Orlov:** Russian oligarch financier.
- **Maverick:** primary antagonist, orchestrating Cedar Crown Phase II.

## Act Breakdown

### Act I – Parthenon Shadows
1. **Return to Athens:** Hunt tracks Leyla’s beacon, clashes with Greek Intelligence, meets Daphne.
2. **Agora Intercept:** Leyla tags Maverick’s courier; Noura recruits Dr. Aris.
3. **Piraeus Whisper:** Shipyard infiltration reveals “Aegean Lock” containers.
4. **Mt. Athos Firewall:** Monastic data haven raid uncovers Cedar Crown accounts.
5. **Jenny’s Debt:** Jenny Stavros joins; Mossad handler Yael demands Samir intel.

### Act II – Island Killbox
6. **Karpathos Drift:** Hunt probes NATO bunker housing micro-reactor.
7. **Temple Run:** Museum gala theft nets naval access badge.
8. **Cyclone over Milos:** Storm battle vs. Cmdr. Nikos’s convoy.
9. **Volcano Vault:** Noura + Aris reroute Santorini geothermal grid.
10. **Delphi Echoes:** Maverick weaponizes Greek myths to turn NIS; Hunt survives ambush.

### Act III – Siege of Crete
11. **Souda Bay Breach:** Joint op with Greek SOF reveals Lev Orlov backing.
12. **Minotaur’s Data:** Daphne decrypts Orlov’s blockchain keys.
13. **Labyrinth Chase:** Heraklion pursuit; Jenny’s sailing saves team from drones.
14. **Aegean Lockdown:** Silo trap; Hunt uses EMP pen to escape.
15. **NIS Mutiny:** Daphne exposes director; Mossad offers truce.

### Act IV – Mykonos Reckoning
16. **Mykonos Night Drop:** HALO insertion to plant charges on Orlov’s yacht.
17. **Windmill Stand:** Rooftop fight amid nightlife; Leyla shields civilians.
18. **Data Torrent:** Maverick’s confession livestreamed via hacked DJ festival.
19. **Poseidon Strike:** Hunt commandeers frigate missile to destroy reactor barge.
20. **Acropolis Dawn:** Wrap-up; Cedar Coalition Athens launch; hook to Morocco.

### Epilogue – Mediterranean Mirage
- Maverick and Orlov surface in Morocco, teeing up Book Three.

## Thematic Beats
- Old empires vs. new tech terror.
- Trust and betrayal within allied intel services.
- Maritime chess—the Mediterranean becomes the board.
- Cedar Coalition as counter-narrative to Cedar Crown.
